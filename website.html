My Interests

My life revolves around school, learning, and.. More learning. I currently go to school at Ohio University and am a Biological Sciences major, and will hopefully be graduating with a Computer Science minor and Bioinformatics Certificate. Being an avid Redditor, I have began searching for graduate and PhD programs of interest. Right now, my goal is Bioinformatics- although, UCSD has a Computational Neuroscience program that may just be my number one. When I am not in school, I am resting and usually talking on the phone with my friends. I have narcolepsy with cataplexy, and have to often 'pay my activities forward' by minimizing stress, trying to get quality sleep, and taking care of my health to the best of my ability. Chronic illness is a circumstance I didn't ask for, but I try my hardest to be an advocate for myself first and foremost, and that requires sometimes being a little uncomfortable.
